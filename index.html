Data Analysis @ UoE LEL

Welcome to the website of the data analysis courses and resources provided by the department of Linguistics and English Language of the University of Edinburgh.

Note that LEL students can also take the courses from Psychology listed in Data Analysis @ UoE Psychology.

Courses

Introduction to Language Research

This course will develop understanding of the process of research, and equip students to make a research contribution in Linguistics and English Language.

Statistics and Experimental Design

An introduction to good design and analysis of scientific studies of behaviour, the course covers issues in experimental design, descriptive statistics, and inferential statistics.

Research Methods in Developmental Linguistics

This course aims to provide students with knowledge and skills in conducting empirical research in developmental linguistics.

Resources

There are several resources you can use to teach yourself data analysis and statistic skills.

Beginners

Statistics for linguists: An introduction using R

This textbook by Bodo Winter is ideal both for absolute beginners and experienced researchers. It has all you need.

R for Data Science

The R4DS free book is an excellent introduction to R and data analysis.

Intermediate/advanced

learnB4SS workshop

The learnB4SS workshop is an introduction to Bayesian analysis for Speech Science.
